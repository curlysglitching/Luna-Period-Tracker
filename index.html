Welcome to Luna ☽
Your personal period & cycle tracker — here’s how everything works.
```
📅 Logging Your Period
👆Tap any day on the calendar to open the day menu.
💧Choose a flow intensity — Light, Medium, or Heavy. The square fills with your chosen color so your cycle is obvious at a glance.
🎨Change your period color anytime using the swatches at the top.
🌸 Symptoms & Notes
🏷Tap any symptom tag — cramps, bloating, fatigue, mood swings, and more.
📝Use the note field for anything extra — medications, observations, or how your day felt.
●A small dot appears on days that have notes or tags logged.
🌸Manually mark an ovulation day if you’ve confirmed it via test or temperature tracking.
🔮 Smart Predictions
🟠Predicted period days show a dashed orange border, calculated from your average cycle.
!A purple ! marks each day of your estimated fertile window (5 days before ovulation) — clean, no background fill.
🌸Your predicted ovulation day gets a purple border — estimated 14 days before your next expected period.
⚡Predictions improve as you log more cycles. They appear once you have at least one full cycle recorded.
📊 Cycle Stats
🔄Avg Cycle — average days between the start of each period.
📅Avg Period — average number of days your period lasts.
🕐Next Period & Ovulation — predicted dates based on your personal history.
📅 Years & Privacy
‹ ›Use the arrows at the top to browse between years. All data is saved across years automatically.
🔒Everything saves to your browser only. Nothing is sent to any server — completely private on your device.
```
